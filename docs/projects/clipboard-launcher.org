* Clipboard and Launcher
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: clipboard-and-launcher
:END:
** Current
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: current
:END:
- Launcher is native Quickshell again. =scripts/launcher-toggle= signals the =qs-launcher= overlay through =$XDG_RUNTIME_DIR=.
- The launcher searches open Hyprland windows and desktop apps from =scripts/launcher-apps=.
- =scripts/launcher-fuzzel= preserves the old fuzzel app launcher as an explicit fallback.
- Clipboard is still script-backed for now: =scripts/clipboard-toggle= uses =cliphist list | fuzzel --dmenu= and decodes the selected item back to =wl-copy=.

** Decisions
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: decisions
:END:
- Keep launcher and clipboard as separate tools until focus restoration, keyboard navigation, and close behavior are boring and reliable.
- Keep explicit fuzzel fallback scripts until the native Quickshell tools are proven boring and reliable.
- Use Quickshell screen binding for layer windows.
- Follow the Quickshell ownership rules: =root/= owns surfaces, =modules/<name>/= owns behavior, =common/= owns reusable primitives only.
- Avoid full-screen invisible masks unless the overlay is intentionally click-out-to-close and the mask is verified not to steal unrelated input.
